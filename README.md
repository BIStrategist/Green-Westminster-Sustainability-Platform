# Green Westminster – Sustainability Engagement Platform

**Timeline:** March 2025  
**Team Size:** 4 Members  
**Role Highlighted:** Team Lead & Analyst

---

## Project Overview
This project focused on designing a **Sustainability Engagement Platform** to promote environmentally responsible behaviour among students. The platform design was guided by stakeholder insights and system modeling.

---

## Contributions
- Led a team of 4 members, coordinating project planning and task allocation
- Conducted **stakeholder analysis and interviews** to gather business and user requirements
- Produced **UML diagrams** including Use Case and Activity Diagrams
- Translated stakeholder insights into **functional and non-functional requirements**
- Delivered a professional **Software Requirements Specification (SRS)**
